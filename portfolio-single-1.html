Restaurant mobile app

Project Date 19th February, 2022
Role Identity, UX Design
Client -
Visit -

As a UX designer to design an app for Katei No Aji restaurant from conception to delivery. My responsibilities areConducting user research, paper and digital wireframing, low and high-fidelity prototyping,conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, logo design, UI design, Art direction and iterating on designs.

The interviews and empathy maps are conducted to help understanding the demand of the users. The user group in the research are people who have different background but both of them like to visit a restaurant with different frequency.

The user group is assumed to be potential customers who have visited the restaurant mobile app. The food menu, booking table system, restaurant transportation information and overall user interface could be the main elements to affect the visitors interest with the restaurant.
