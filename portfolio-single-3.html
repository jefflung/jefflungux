Letting property responsive website

Project Date 9th April, 2022
Role Identity, UX Design
Client -
Visit -

As a UX designer, I was responsible for all design of UKRent website. My responsibilities are Conducting interviews, paper and digital wireframing, low and high-fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, iterating on designs and responsive design.

I conducted user interviews, which I then turned into empathy maps to better understand the target user and their needs. I discovered that many target users visit online letting agency website when they have plan to move out. On the other hand, some users visit this kind of websites in their leisure time for the sake of updating knowledge of existing properties in market. However, many letting websites are overwhelming with content which is not arranged with a clear structure. As a result, this frustrated many target users and led a normally enjoyable experience to become challenging for them, defeating a good user experience.
