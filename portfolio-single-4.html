As a programming learner, I can empathy that programmers always struggle to find a way to match with other programmers. Their objectives may be for practice of coding, freelance projects or long term employment. Project overview. My goal is designing an app for programmers to search and match with other programmers easily.

I used Pair Codersâ€™s data on matching programming partners to develop interview questions, which were then used to conduct user interviews. Most Interview participants reported feeling badly about searching partners for programming, because they are lack of effective ways to achieve this goal. The feedback received through research made it very clear that users would be looking forward to using a dedicated tool for searching partners for pair programming.
